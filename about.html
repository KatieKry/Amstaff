About Amstaffs

American Staffordshire Terriers, also known as American Staffy or AmStaffs, is a medium-sized, short-haired American dog breed. The American Kennel Club describes the breed as "confident, smart and good-natured."

Physical Traits

AmStaffs are stocky, muscular bull-type terriers standing 17 to 19 inches at the shoulder. The head is broad, the jaws well defined, the cheekbones pronounced, and the dark, round eyes are set wide apart. AmStaff movement is agile and graceful, with a springy gait that advertises the breed's innate confidence. The stiff, glossy coat comes in many colors and patterns.

Personality Traits

AmStaffs are cognizant of their surroundings, willing to try anything, and have the most lovable personalities. They are easily trainable and are known as the "nanny" breed because of their desire to take care of and monitor the children in a family. Although, this breed is considered part of the Pitbull or "Bully" family, they are the sweetest dogs around. If you bring this breed into your home, they will show loyalty, love, and affection 24/7.

Why We Love Staffies

Amstaffs are the biggest babies you will ever meet. They tend to follow you whereever you may go and will try to lay as close to you as possible at all times. They have a lot of love to give and are not shy when it comes to showing that love to other people. They will trust you 100% and do whatever you ask, even if you have to ask 10 times. They are a great family dog and will be there for you to snuggle, love, and play with whenever you want. We love Staffies!
